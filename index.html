Energy Consumption Data Visualization

Interactive analysis of TV energy consumption and electricity spot prices

1. Energy Consumption vs Star Rating

This scatter plot reveals the relationship between TV energy star ratings and actual power consumption. Higher star ratings generally indicate better energy efficiency and lower consumption.

2. Energy Consumption by Screen Technology

A donut chart showing the total energy consumption distribution across different screen technologies (LED, LCD, Plasma, OLED) for all TV sizes combined.

3. 55-inch TV Energy by Screen Type

Comparing energy consumption patterns specifically for 55-inch televisions across different screen technologies to understand efficiency differences at a standard size.

4. Electricity Spot Prices (1998-2024)

Historical electricity spot price trends across Australian states, showing price volatility and regional variations over nearly three decades.
